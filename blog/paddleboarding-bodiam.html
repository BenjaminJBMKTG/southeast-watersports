Common Questions

Do I need to be fit or have experience?

Not at all! Paddleboarding is suitable for all fitness levels and we welcome complete beginners. Our instructors tailor each session to your ability, so everyone can enjoy it at their own pace.

What should I bring?

Just bring a towel, swimwear, and a change of clothes. We provide everything else: board, paddle, buoyancy aid, and wetsuit if needed.

What if the weather's bad?

We paddle in most weather conditions, but safety comes first. If conditions aren't safe, we'll contact you to reschedule. We want you to have a brilliant experience!

Can children take part?

Yes! We're family-friendly and children are welcome. We recommend checking our booking page for age requirements on specific sessions.
