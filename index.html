Our Locations

Bodiam

River Rother

Paddle past the iconic Bodiam Castle on the serene River Rother. Perfect for beginners and those seeking a peaceful, scenic experience in the heart of the Sussex countryside.

Hastings Beach

Coastal Adventures

Experience the thrill of sea kayaking and paddleboarding on the stunning Sussex coast. Weather-dependent sessions offer an exhilarating challenge for intermediate paddlers.

Tonbridge

River Medway

Explore the historic River Medway, winding through picturesque Kent countryside. Great for all abilities with calm waters and beautiful scenery year-round.
