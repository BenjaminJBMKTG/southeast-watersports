Kayaking Adventures

Explore Kent & Sussex waterways from a different perspective

Experience the Thrill of Kayaking

Kayaking is the perfect way to explore our beautiful rivers, coastline, and hidden waterways. Whether you're seeking a peaceful paddle through historic landscapes or an adrenaline-pumping sea kayaking adventure, we have the perfect session for you.

Our kayaking sessions cater to all abilities, from complete beginners to experienced paddlers. We provide everything you need - kayaks, paddles, spraydeck, wetsuits, and buoyancy aids - so you can focus on enjoying the experience.

With qualified instructors who know these waters intimately, you'll learn proper technique, safety procedures, and discover the rich history and wildlife of the South East's waterways. Small group sizes ensure everyone gets the attention they need to progress and enjoy their time on the water.

Our Kayaking Sessions

Taster & Adventure Sessions

2.5 - 3 hours

Your introduction to kayaking! These sessions are designed for complete beginners and families looking to try something new. Start with a comprehensive safety briefing and on-land instruction, then take to the water to practice your paddling technique.

Choose from our stunning locations: glide past the historic Bodiam Castle on the River Rother, explore the tranquil River Medway at Tonbridge, or experience the unique atmosphere of paddling through Kent and Sussex countryside.

  • No experience necessary
  • Full instruction provided
  • All equipment included
  • Suitable for ages 8+
  • Small groups (max 8)
  • Choice of scenic locations

Sea Kayaking

3 hours

Take on the challenge of the open water! Our sea kayaking sessions at Hastings Beach offer an exhilarating experience for those with some kayaking experience. Navigate coastal waters, learn about tides and currents, and develop your skills in a more dynamic environment.

This is a step up from our river sessions and requires confidence on the water. You'll learn advanced techniques for dealing with waves, wind, and changing conditions while enjoying the stunning Sussex coastline.

  • Previous kayaking experience recommended
  • Weather-dependent (safety first!)
  • Advanced techniques taught
  • Coastal navigation skills
  • All specialist sea kayaking equipment provided
  • Suitable for ages 12+

Why Choose Kayaking?

Great Exercise

Low-impact full-body workout that builds strength, endurance, and core stability while having fun on the water.

Connect with Nature

Get up close to wildlife, explore hidden waterways, and experience the beauty of the South East from a unique perspective.

Mindful Adventure

The rhythmic motion of paddling and the tranquil surroundings create a meditative experience that reduces stress.

For Everyone

From gentle family paddles to challenging sea adventures, there's a kayaking session for every age and ability level.

Explore Historic Waterways

Our kayaking locations have been carefully selected to offer both stunning scenery and fascinating history:

  • Bodiam, River Rother: Paddle past the iconic 14th-century Bodiam Castle, one of England's most picturesque fortifications. The gentle current and beautiful countryside make this perfect for beginners.
  • Tonbridge, River Medway: Wind through historic Kent countryside on one of the region's major rivers. Spot wildlife, explore quiet backwaters, and enjoy the peaceful rural setting.
  • Hastings Beach: For the more adventurous, our sea kayaking sessions offer the thrill of coastal paddling with views of the famous Hastings coastline and its dramatic cliffs.
